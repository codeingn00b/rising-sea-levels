Causes

Rising sea levels are mainly caused by global warming, melting ice caps, and thermal expansion of seawater.

Effects

Flooding, loss of habitats, and stronger coastal storms threaten millions of people and ecosystems worldwide.

Actions

We can reduce carbon emissions, invest in renewable energy, and protect coastal ecosystems.

Data

NASA reports sea levels are rising about 3.3 mm per year globally.
